AIRCRAFT · SUB-250G CLASS
Per 14 CFR Part 48, aircraft <250 g flown recreationally do not require FAA registration. TRUST is still required.

Top Rules · § 44809 + FTCA

  • Recreational only — no compensation, no work.
  • VLOS — pilot or co-located VO maintains visual line of sight unaided.
  • ≤400 ft AGL in Class G uncontrolled airspace.
  • Controlled airspace (B, C, D, E surface) — LAANC required.
  • Yield to manned aircraft — always. Right of way is theirs.
  • No flight over people directly.
  • 25 ft from other pilots · 50 ft from spectators.
  • FPV requires VO with VLOS.
  • TRUST on demand — show to FAA / law enforcement.

Emergency · Incident

  • Land immediately if a safe landing is possible.
  • If not, divert away from people, vehicles, structures.
  • Ensure everyone is safe · address injuries first.
  • Report to local FTCA representative.
  • Consider reporting to NASA sUAS ASRS at asrs.arc.nasa.gov/uassafety.html
  • Common triggers: lost link, instrument fail, weather, parts breaking, battery fire, unanticipated traffic.

IMSAFE

  • Illness · symptoms affecting ops?
  • Medication · prescription or otherwise?
  • Stress · psychological/emotional factors?
  • Alcohol · none in last 8 hr (24 hr full metabolization).
  • Fatigue · adequate rest?
  • Emotion · emotionally upset?
